FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions about Thundrr & the TikTok LIVE + Shop Agency Program

What are the benefits of joining your TikTok LIVE Agency?

• Keep all your diamonds. We don’t take a cut
• Direct support from the TikTok team, including unban support & account loss prevention
• First-hand benefits from TikTok – TikTok hosted tournaments, campaigns and spotlight opportunities
• Access to a private network of high quality agency creators
• Personalized strategy to increase viewership and diamonds through 1:1 meetings
• You will be assigned to a dedicated creator manager to assist you any day of the week

What are the benefits of joining your TikTok Shop program?

• Direct support from TikTok Shop
• Assigned a dedicated TikTok Shop manager & point of contact
• Exclusive brand deals and opportunities directly from TikTok Shop & Thundrr (paid streaming opportunities, exclusive products, collaborate directly with brands outside of Shop)
• Ban/Violation support incase your Shop permissions get taken down/revoked for violating guidelines
• First hand insight on the best selling products as well as coaching from TikTok & our Agency to scale your Shop and revenue

Is the Agency program entirely free?

The TikTok LIVE Agency program is entirely free to creators! We are incentivized by TikTok to coach and assist you on LIVE. There are virtually no downsides of joining the Agency as it is free forever, the program was put in place to help support creators that create higher quality content on the platform.

How do I find my TikTok LIVE Agency Code?

(Head over to your profile and click the top right where your settings are > Creator Tools > LIVE Center > Agency Center)

What can I expect once I join Thundrr?

Once you join Thundrr your dedicated creator manager will reach out to you and schedule a one on one meeting that can be done over a call or via messages to help get you started on your LIVE journey!
